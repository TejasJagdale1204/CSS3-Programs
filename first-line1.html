Sports and games are crucial for a student's balanced development, offering more than just physical activity. They cultivate vital life skills like teamwork, leadership, and discipline, teaching students to adhere to rules and manage both victory and defeat with grace. Engaging in sports also builds confidence, enhances mental alertness, and fosters perseverance. These habits and values learned on the field are invaluable, preparing students to navigate adult life with greater resilience and well-roundedness, proving sports are an integral part of a holistic education.
